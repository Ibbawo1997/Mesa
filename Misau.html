New Leadership Sworn In

The Misau Emirate Students Association (MESA) National Body has officially sworn in Comr. Usman Abdullahi Usman as its National President, alongside other newly elected executive officers.

A press release made available to newsmen by the Publicity Secretary of the Association, Comrade Musa Muhammad Bakoji on Monday, said the inauguration came following a peaceful and transparent national convention and election held on Monday at MESA National Secretariat, Fawari Primary School, Misau, Bauchi State.

About MESA

Learn about our mission, history, and the impact we're making in the lives of students from Misau Emirate.

Connecting Students, Building Futures

Misau Emirate Students Association (MESA) is an incorporated trustee established in 2012 to promote the welfare, education, and cultural connection of students from Misau Emirate.

Our association connects students across campuses, organizes educational and cultural events, and supports academic initiatives to ensure students from our emirate achieve their full potential.

Our Objectives

The core goals that drive our activities and initiatives for the benefit of Misau Emirate students.

Support Education

Provide scholarships, mentoring, and academic support for students originating from Misau Emirate to ensure their academic success.

Preserve Culture

Promote cultural activities and maintain strong ties with Misau Emirate traditions, values, and community leaders.

Networking

Connect students across universities and colleges to share opportunities, internships, career guidance, and social events.

Community Development

Engage in community service projects and contribute to the educational and social development of Misau Emirate.
